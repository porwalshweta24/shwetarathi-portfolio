October, 2023

Hyderabad-Srisailam
Hyderabad, Telangana

Embarking on a long weekend getaway, we planned a three-day road trip from Pune to Hyderabad, eager for an adventure filled with exploration and discovery. Our destination? The awe-inspiring Statue of Equality, a monumental tribute to humanity's unity and diversity.

As we journeyed through the diverse landscapes of southern India, our excitement mounted with each passing mile. The anticipation of experiencing the grandeur of the Statue of Equality and the spiritual tranquility of Hyderabad filled us with eager anticipation. Along the way, we shared stories, laughter, and snacks, forging deeper bonds as we traversed the scenic route towards our destination.

Arriving in Hyderabad on the second day of our long weekend, our first stop was the Statue of Equality, nestled within the serene surroundings of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) temple complex. Standing majestically at over 216 feet tall, the statue depicted the revered saint, philosopher, and social reformer, Sri Ramanujacharya. As we marveled at its grandeur, we felt a sense of awe and reverence wash over us, reminding us of the enduring values of love and compassion that transcend all boundaries.

In addition to visiting the Statue of Equality, we had the privilege of experiencing the sacred ritual of Abhishek at the Mallikarjun Temple. This ancient practice, where devotees offer sacred substances like milk, honey, and water to the deity, was a profoundly moving experience. As we participated in the ritual, we felt a deep sense of connection to the divine and to the rich spiritual heritage of our country.

But our long weekend journey didn't end there. Before departing Hyderabad, we ventured into the sanctuary of a jungle area allowed for daytime travel. Amidst the lush greenery and vibrant wildlife, we encountered the serene beauty of nature firsthand. Along the way, we indulged in local delicacies like ram kand phal, savoring the flavors of the region. These moments of exploration and immersion in nature added a sense of adventure and wonder to our long weekend getaway, leaving us with memories to cherish for a lifetime.

Sept, 2023

Dwarika-Somnath
Dwarika-Nageshwar-Somnath, Gujrat

In the sacred month of Sravan Adhik Maas in 2023, our group of close friends embarked on a special pilgrimage Gujrat jyotilings. Spanning from the 11th to the 15th of August, our journey coincided with a long weekend, providing us with ample time to immerse ourselves in spiritual experiences and create lasting memories. With great anticipation, we planned a five-day trip from Pune to explore the sacred sites of Jyotirling Somnath and Nageshwar, followed by the mystical city of Dwarika and its revered Bhet Dwarika.

Our adventure commenced with a flight from Pune to Mumbai, where we rendezvoused before continuing our travels to Rajkot by air, and then by car to the sacred city of Dwarika. En route, we eagerly anticipated the spiritual revelations that awaited us, knowing that our journey fell within the sacred time frame of Sravan month Adhik Maas, believed to amplify the blessings of religious activities.

Upon our arrival in Dwarika, we were greeted by the ancient charm and spiritual aura of the city, steeped in mythology and legend. Our first stop was the enchanting Bhet Dwarika, accessible by a scenic ferry ride across the Arabian Sea. As we approached the island, the sight of the temple complex, surrounded by azure waters, left us in awe of its divine beauty.

After paying our respects at Bhet Dwarika, we embarked on a soul-stirring journey to the revered Jyotirling Somnath Temple. Set against the backdrop of the Arabian Sea, the temple's majestic architecture and serene ambiance provided the perfect setting for moments of prayer and reflection. As we witnessed the mesmerizing evening aarti ceremony, the rhythmic chants and the flickering lamps created an atmosphere of divine grace and spiritual enlightenment.

Mount Abu
Mount Abu, Rajasthan

Embarking on a three-day expedition from Pune to the serene heights of Mount Abu, our journey promised a blend of spiritual exploration and natural wonder. With a packed itinerary, we eagerly set off to discover the gems of Nakki Lake, the intricacies of Delwara Temples, the divine presence at Amba Ji, and the breathtaking vistas from Gabbar via an exhilarating ropeway ride.

Our adventure began as we arrived in Mount Abu, where we wasted no time in heading straight to Nakki Lake. Enveloped by verdant landscapes, the lake's tranquil waters provided the perfect setting for a leisurely boat ride. As we drifted along, we were captivated by the serenity of the surroundings, setting a peaceful tone for the rest of our journey.

The following day, we delved into the spiritual heart of Mount Abu with a visit to the renowned Delwara Temples. Renowned for their intricate architecture and sacred atmosphere, each temple exuded a sense of divine presence. The intricate carvings and rich history left us in awe, offering a glimpse into the spiritual heritage of the region. After paying our respects, we proceeded to Amba Ji, where we immersed ourselves in the spiritual aura of the temple, seeking blessings for our journey ahead amidst the tranquil ambiance of the Aravalli Range.

Our final day in Mount Abu was marked by an exhilarating ascent to Gabbar via the ropeway. As we soared to the summit, the panoramic views of the surrounding landscape took our breath away. At the top, we were greeted by sweeping vistas of Mount Abu's majestic peaks and valleys, a fitting finale to our adventure. With hearts full of gratitude and memories to cherish, we bid farewell to Mount Abu, knowing that our three-day journey had been a truly enriching and unforgettable experience.

Mehsana
Mehsana, Gujrat

Embarking on a road trip from Chittor to Mehsana, our family of parents and teenage siblings set out for a day of adventure at Shankus Water Park. As we hit the open road, excitement filled the air, and the anticipation of a thrilling day ahead kept us all in high spirits.

The journey was as much a part of the experience as the destination itself. We passed through scenic landscapes, exchanging stories and laughter along the way. The road trip offered us precious moments of bonding, strengthening the familial ties that bound us together.

Upon reaching Shankus Water Park, we were greeted by a world of exhilarating rides and refreshing pools. From heart-pounding water slides to lazy river floats, there was something for everyone to enjoy. The joy on our parents' faces as they joined in the fun with us teenagers added an extra layer of happiness to the day.

As the sun began to set, we reluctantly bid farewell to Shankus Water Park, our hearts full of cherished memories from a day well spent. The road trip back to Chittor was filled with contented silence, each of us lost in our own reflections of the wonderful time we had shared together.
